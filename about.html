About


Founded in September 2016, Blue Balloon Theatre acts as a platform for actors and creatives wanting to develop and showcase their own work. Rebecca's aim was and is to instil confidence in artists and their writing with a support network and facilities behind them that can make it happen.


In a just over a year, Blue Balloon Theatre celebrated diversity and passion within a variety of artists through public events, play development workshops, promoting undiscovered talent and managed to raise Â£152 for a charitable cause.


As of November 2017, Artistic Director, Rebecca, began co-running the company with fellow northern actor and best friend, Jasmine Nisic, Blue Balloon's appointed Associate Director. Jasmine is politically driven with a passion for diversity.


2018 has shaped up to be a very exciting year indeed! Blue Balloon delved into the wonder of spoken word and poetry, hosting several open mic nights which proved to be consitently brilliant and inspirational for all involved! It is through this work in poetry that Blue Balloon accrued the third and newest member of the team, Louise Croft! Louise is a lover of unicorns, poetry and nice lippy! She has worked with Rebecca and Jasmine continuously for over a year on various Blue Balloon events and is very much intune with the ethos they try to uphold. Their future together is looking for bright!


As of December 2018, Blue Balloon Theatre transitioned from a non-profit organization to a limited company. The fierce trio are thrilled and excited to see where 2019 takes them, with the hope of theatre productions and more events on the horizon!

Louise Croft

Marketing Executive

Louise is an aspiring poet and writer, who studied English with Creative Writing at the University of Nottingham. Her poetry usually features mentions of her urban surroundings in Manchester and her experience of disappointing relationships. Louise tries to fill her poetry with honesty and hope and a comic spin.

She loves all things, pink, sparkly and unicorn related and would like to encourage more people to wear glitter on a daily basis. As well as the art of writing, Louise also adores theatre and has helped out backstage on several theatre productions whilst at university. She is thrilled to be a part of Blue Balloon, as her goals in life are to spread the arts, making them more accessible, and encouraging and supporting new artists to emerge.
